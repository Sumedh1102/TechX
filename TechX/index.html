TECH X 2.0

About Us

Welcome to Tech X, the official fest of St. John College of Engineering and Management, proudly presented by the CSI Committee! This vibrant celebration of innovation and creativity unites students through exciting cultural performances, technical workshops, and engaging events. Join us to ignite curiosity, showcase talent, and create unforgettable memories in an extraordinary journey of passion and discovery!

Journey Through Events

Day 1

kicks off with the thrilling Escape Room challenge from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM in Hall A, where participants solve puzzles to escape within a time limit. The day wraps up with the Tech Quiz in the Seminar Hall from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM, testing participants’ technical expertise.

Day 2

This Day is all about gaming excitement with the BGMI Tournament from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM in the Gaming Zone, as squads battle for the coveted chicken dinner.

Day 3

begins with the Mini Militia Tournament from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM, also in the Gaming Zone, offering a fun multiplayer shootout. The afternoon features the Tech Debate in the Seminar Hall from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM, where participants engage in thought-provoking discussions on tech innovations.

Day 4

The final day of the event series is dedicated to Design Alchemy, held from 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the Design Studio. This activity provides participants with an inspiring platform to showcase their creativity and innovative design skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

TECH x 2.0 is the annual college fest of St. John College of Engineering and Management, a thrilling event filled with cultural performances, technical competitions, workshops, and more! It’s an incredible platform to showcase your skills, meet like-minded people, and have an amazing time.

The fest will be held from January 15 to January 17, 2024, at the St. John College of Engineering and Management campus. Check the Timeline section for detailed schedules.

TECH x 2.0 is open to students of St. John College of Engineering and Management, as well as participants from other colleges. Event-specific eligibility details are available on the Events page.

Registration is simple and online. Visit the Events page, select the event you want to participate in, and complete the registration form. Make sure to register early!

Some events may require a small registration fee, while others are free. Fee details are mentioned in the event descriptions on the website.
